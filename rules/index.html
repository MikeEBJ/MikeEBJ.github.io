Minecraft Server Rules

Please follow these rules to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone. Use common sense, be respectful, and keep the world beautiful.

1. Don't attack others

Always get permission before attacking someone.

2. Owning land

You can’t just claim land without reason.

3. Don't grief

Don't mess with other people's stuff.

4. Don’t grief the overworld

Don't spam nerd poles or leave creeper holes. Clean up after yourself. Applies loosely in the Nether.

5. Don’t destroy trial chambers

Don’t break vaults or remove spawners in trial chambers.

6. Respect others

Respect when someone asks you to leave. Don’t harass others.

7. Public farms

Farms are public unless clearly marked private.

8. Banned players’ belongings

Items go to staff. Shared bases get handled via ticket.

These rules are subject to change. By playing on the server, you agree to follow them. Contact staff if you need clarification.
